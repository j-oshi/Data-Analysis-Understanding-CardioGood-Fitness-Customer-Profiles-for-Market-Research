def categorize_restaurants(restaurant_type):
    if ('bar' in restaurant_type.lower()) or ('pub' in restaurant_type.lower()):
        return 'pub'
    elif ('takeway' in restaurant_type.lower()):
        return 'takeaway'
    elif ('delivery' in restaurant_type.lower()):
        return 'delivery'
    elif ('restaurant' in restaurant_type.lower()):
        return 'restaurant'         
    else:
        return 'others'
    

postcode_df['category_by_primary_service'] = postcode_df['category'].apply(categorize_restaurants)

3 Summary of the Analysis

3.1. Distribution of Postcode in Portsmouth Graphs¶

  1. Distribution of Restaurants by Outward Code - Pie Chart (Left):

    • Displays the distribution of restaurants by outward codes (postal areas).
    • Most restaurants are located in PO1 (44%) and PO5 (33.6%), highlighting these as the densest areas for restaurants.
    • Other areas like PO2, PO4, PO6, PO3, and PO12 have significantly smaller shares, ranging from 0.9% to 8.6%.

  2. Distribution of Restaurants across Postcodes - Bar Chart (Right):

    • Depicts the count of restaurants across postcodes.
    • PO1 and PO5 dominate in terms of restaurant counts, whereas PO12 and PO3 have the lowest.
    • This further confirms PO1 and PO5 as central hubs for restaurants.

  3. Distribution of Restaurants by Primary Service - Bar Chart (Left):

    • Primary Service Categories: Includes "restaurant," "pub," "others," and "delivery."
    • Dominance of Restaurants: The "restaurant" category overwhelmingly dominates, with over 80 establishments, far surpassing other categories.
    • Secondary Categories: The "pub" and "others" categories follow, each contributing around 15-20 establishments. The "delivery" category has the least representation.

  4. Distribution of Restaurants by Primary Service by Postcode - Bar Chart (Right):
